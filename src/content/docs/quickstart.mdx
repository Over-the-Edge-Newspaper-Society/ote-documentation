## Getting Started in 5 Minutes

<Steps>
1. **Review the Style Guide** 
   Start with our [Writing Standards](/style-guide/writing-standards/) to understand our editorial voice and conventions.

2. **Understand Our Process** 
   Check the [Story Workflow](/editorial-process/story-workflow/) to see how articles move from pitch to publication.

3. **Access Resources** 
   Download [Templates](/resources/templates/) you'll need for writing and submission.

4. **Submit Your First Article** 
   Follow our [submission guidelines](/editorial-process/story-workflow/) to get your work into our pipeline.
</Steps>

## Quick Reference Checklist

Before submitting any content, ensure you've:

- [ ] Read our [Writing Standards](/style-guide/writing-standards/)
- [ ] Followed AP Style guidelines in our [Style Guide](/style-guide/ap-style/)
- [ ] Used the appropriate [Article Template](/resources/templates/)
- [ ] Checked against our [Voice and Tone guidelines](/style-guide/voice-and-tone/)
- [ ] Reviewed the [Story Workflow](/editorial-process/story-workflow/) process
